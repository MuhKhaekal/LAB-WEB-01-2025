About the Album

Puting Beliung is Tenxi’s latest album, released in August 2025. With 13 dynamic tracks, the record introduces Hipdut, a groundbreaking fusion of hip-hop and dangdut. This album blends infectious beats, raw emotions, and cultural rhythm, delivering a fresh sound that storms through today’s music scene.
